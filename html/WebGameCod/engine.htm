¿HOW THE VIDEOGAMES ENGINE WORKS?


During the practice, various activities will be carried out in the indicated areas, participants will have an introduction to video game engines and how they work.


After this introduction, they will be led more explicitly to the specific Unity engine, so that they can later carry out the practice that will be carried out in the classroom with computers.


Once in practice, the participants will be able to carry out a first program, with help, with which they will create a capsule that can move through a plane, in this way they will have a first contact with the engine and the most basic parts of it.
