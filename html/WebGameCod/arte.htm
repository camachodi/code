Art in videogames


¿HOW THE ART IN VIDEOGAMES HAS BEEN MADE?


In the Introduction to Art in Video Games workshop we are going to, firstly, define what it is and belongs to art in the industry, why it is that is defined that way and how do we know if any particular concept should be regarded as such. Then, give a quick look at how the history of art in video games unfolded since its birth in the 1970s until today giving special attention to the so called 5 generations or eras in video game art.


We will distinguish between the many different disciplines and responsibilities within development in the industry and, finally, give examples of development in each one, naming the tools and resources artists need to create assets for any game, giving special attention to the required skills and purpose of each one of them. Hoping that this way those who attend the workshop will have a solid understanding of art in video games and how to start learning on their own.
