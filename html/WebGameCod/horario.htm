¿HOW THE ORGANIZATION HAS BEEN MADE?


The organization of this event has been made by taking all the activity of each workshop. The first thing that we needed to do was schedule the hours of each workshop activity, that way we would be able to fit each of them into schedule time.


Of course, time isn’t the only thing that we have to pay attention to, we have to determine the physical requirements of each workshop, for example Video Game Programming Workshop needs good computers, so at the list of workshops it says the physical site to work in. Later at the schedule we needed to fit all the workshops at his hours and at the physical site.


Putting everything together we noticed that even if we had into the workshop breaks, actually the breaks it does by the separation of the workshops themselves into the schedule so we didn’t need them. Also the workshops have been differentiated with colors.
