¿HOW GROUPS DINAMICS WORK?


Throughout these classes we will teach the students the importance of co-working and some key points of it. Furthermore, they will experience themselves what co-working is and why it is so important for their jobs and both personal and professional lives and its benefit.


The session will start with a bit of theory about Group Dynamics which is based in following Tuckman’s Model and showing the different roles and forces than can be found in a work group. In addition, some useful tools and tips will be introduced in order to avoid negative atmospheres when working.


Following the theory, the organisation will do some activities for the participants to start interacting with each other.


After introducing each other to the rest of the group, the participants will do some teamwork activities to enhance their co-working skills. It is not important whether they have co-worked before or not, since these activities will teach them all in a proper way.


The last stage will be a yoga class. If co-working is important, taking enough time to relax is also really important. People are not machines and they do need to rest and relax in order to obtain better results.
